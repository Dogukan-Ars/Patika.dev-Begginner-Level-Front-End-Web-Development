Tokyo is the Capital City of Japan. And also Tokyo is the most populous in the world. Tokyo's population is 37,435 million.

Akihabara where has so much fun and amusement centers is the district of Tokyo. You can find every kind of anime, manga, figures, game and cosplay stuff in here.

Shibuya Square is the most crowded crossing in the world. 1,5 million pedestrians across the Shibuya crossing in a week.
